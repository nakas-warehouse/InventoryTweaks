[h1]1. About[/h1]
[b]Inventory Tweaks[/b] is a client-side mod which adds several configurable tweaks to the inventory, including new visual animations, refined mouse interactions, etc.

[h1]2. Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] [b]Quick-shift[/b]: Hold Shift to interact with slots continuously.

[*] [b]Quick-control[/b]: Hold Control to interact with slots continuously.

[*] [b]Stack Refill[/b]: Replaces used items with new stacks from inventory.

[*] [b]Mouse Refill[/b]: Refills the item in-hand with inventory stacks.

[*] [b]Distribution[/b]: Hold right-click and hover to deposit items.

[*] [b]Stack Amount[/b]: Set how many items to take from a stack.
[/list]

[h1]3. Compatibility[/h1]
[list]
[*] [b]Magic Storage[/b]

[*] [b]Dragon Lens[/b]
[/list]
